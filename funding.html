Funding for ASPECT

Like many other open source project, a significant part of ASPECT has been written either as a by-product for other projects people were funded for, or by people on university-funded positions. We are very grateful for this support!

However, beyond this, ASPECT has also been explicitly supported by projects dedicated to this one code. In particular, we acknowledge the following sources of support with great gratitude:

Computational Infrastructure in Geodynamics initiative (CIG), through the National Science Foundation under Awards No. EAR-0949446, EAR-1550901, and EAR-2149126 via The University of California - Davis.

Award OCI-1148116.
Award EAR-1925595, see the project website at integrated-earth.github.io.
Award OAC-2410845, see the project website at landlab-aspect.github.io
    Helmholtz Association and the GEOMAR Helmholtz Centre for Ocean Research Kiel through the Joint Initiative for Research and Innovation: Funding of first-time professorial appointments of excellent women scientists.
